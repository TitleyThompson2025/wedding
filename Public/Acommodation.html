Here are some suggestions of local accomodation, although this is not exhaustive. There are also Airbnbs and campsites in the area.


Camping at Two Woods Estate

Distance: on site.

Provide your own tent and please ensure this is set up before the ceremony.

There will be quiet drinks around the camp fire after the event.

Only those camping will be able to stay on site after 11.30pm.

Camping is charged at £30 per person to the venue.

Please include in your RSVP if you intend to camp as we need to provide a list.
